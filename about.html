Fragrance of nature was founded in 2017 by Andile Sasanti. The business was originally called Fragrance of nature and managed by Andile. As a new company, we are creating a modern 100% all natural cleaning industry with environmentally friendly products. Service excellence is a key to our success and we continue to expand our service nationwide. After noticing a gap in the South African market for a reliable and efficient cleaning service, Fragrance of nature was formed. We are a proud supplier of Floorwiz assuring innovation in cleaning equipment and eco-green products to create a safer and clean environment. Our cleaning solution is not only uncompromisingly dependable and safe, but also a formula that delivers the best hygiene results.

Fragrance of nature offers the most detailed home cleaning service and the most advanced equipment to guarantee a happy clean home.

All of our cleaning services are tailor made to suite our clients cleaning specifications. Our clients are always welcome to specify their exact requirements when it comes to domestic cleaning service. We also offer an array additional cleaning service, from specialized upholstery and carpet cleaning services to the cleaning of post events/ functions as we have the ideal cleaning solution required.
